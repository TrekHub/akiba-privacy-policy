📄 Privacy Policy – Akiba App

Effective Date: April 8, 2025

🔐 1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve the Akiba experience:

  • Personal Data: Name and email address (only if signed in with Firebase Authentication), device type, OS version, and app version.
  • Financial Data: Income entries, expense details, and budget information manually input by the user.

Note: We do not store sensitive financial data such as bank account or card details.

☁️ 2. How We Use Your Data

  • To store and display your financial data securely via Firebase Firestore.
  • To provide personalized insights and data visualizations.
  • To enhance app performance and resolve bugs.

🔒 3. Data Security

  • Data stored in Google Firebase with industry-standard encryption.
  • All data transmitted over secure HTTPS connections.
  • No data shared with third parties without user consent.

🚫 4. What We Don’t Do

  • No selling, renting, or sharing of personal data for marketing.
  • No access to contacts, camera, location, or SMS unless permission is granted for a specific feature.

🧽 5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is kept as long as you use Akiba. Upon account deletion, your data is permanently erased.

To delete your account, email us at support@akibaapp.com or use the in-app "Delete Account" feature (if available).

📍 6. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication, database, crash analytics
  • Syncfusion Charts – for visual data representation (no personal data is shared)

See the Firebase Privacy Policy.

👶 7. Children’s Privacy

Akiba is not designed for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

🌍 8. Your Rights

  • Access or correct personal data we hold
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Contact us at support@akibaapp.com to exercise your rights.
